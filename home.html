Memory Game

Objective: Match pairs of cards with the same image to win the game.

  • All cards are placed face down at the beginning.
  • Click on two cards at a time to reveal them.
  • If the two revealed cards are a match, they stay face up.
  • If they don't match, they are turned back face down.
  • Try to match all pairs with the fewest moves possible.

Sudoku

Objective: Fill in the grid with numbers from 1 to 9, ensuring that each number appears only once in each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid.

  • The grid consists of 9x9 cells divided into 3x3 subgrids.
  • Some cells are pre-filled with numbers, and your task is to fill in the remaining cells.
  • Each number from 1 to 9 must appear only once in each row, column, and subgrid.
  • The puzzle is solved when all the cells are filled correctly.
  • There is only one correct solution for each Sudoku puzzle.

Tic-Tac-Toe

Objective: Be the first player to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal.

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns, one using X and the other using O.
  • Players take turns placing their marks in an empty cell on the grid.
  • The first player to align three of their marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.
  • If all cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.

EndPoint Pursuit

Objective: Navigate through obstacles and reach the red endpoint while managing your remaining moves and lives.

  • The player starts at the top-left corner of the grid.
  • A red endpoint is located at the bottom-right corner.
  • The grid is filled with static obstacles, and some obstacles move periodically.
  • Use the arrow keys to move the player on the grid.
  • Each move consumes one of your remaining moves, which are limited.
  • Colliding with obstacles will cost you a life. You start with three lives.
  • If you reach the endpoint without running out of moves or lives, you win the game!
  • If you run out of moves or lives, the game is over.
  • Try to reach the endpoint with the fewest moves possible!
